What's new in 2.9:

• Share any tour with friends — new Share button on every tour detail screen uses a waipoint.o11r.com/tour/... link that opens directly in the app (or falls back to Safari if they don't have it installed).
• Passenger Mode — new simplified playback view with larger text, manual next/previous, and no map or GPS. Perfect for someone in the passenger seat or reading along at home. Reachable via the new "Passenger Mode" button or from shared /passenger/<id> links.
• Offline-aware sharing — if you've downloaded the tour locally, shared links still work instantly.

Please test:
• Open any tour → tap Share → send the link to yourself. Tapping the link should open the tour in the app (requires the apple-app-site-association file to be deployed on waipoint.o11r.com; until then, the share link opens in Safari).
• Open any tour → tap Passenger Mode → big play/pause/next/previous controls work, text is readable at arm's length.
• Paste a link of the form https://waipoint.o11r.com/passenger/<shareId> into Messages and tap — should jump straight into Passenger Mode.
