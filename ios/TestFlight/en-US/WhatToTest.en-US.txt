What's new in 2.12:

• Smoother generation spinner — the "Generating your tour" screen now uses a TimelineView-driven compass + animated progress ring that always renders, plus a live "seconds elapsed" counter.
• Keep going in background — after 10 seconds of generating, a "Keep going in background — I'll notify you" button appears. Tap it to dismiss the overlay and keep exploring the rest of the app. A local notification fires when the tour is ready.
• Top-of-screen status banner reminds you that a build is still running in the background so you don't forget.

Please test:
• Generate any tour and observe the spinner — the compass should rotate smoothly and the progress ring should fill over ~20 seconds
• Watch the "seconds elapsed" counter tick up after 5 seconds
• Wait past 10 seconds → the "Keep going in background" button should slide in — tap it
• The overlay should dismiss and a small status banner should appear at the top of Home
• When the tour finishes, you should get a notification (grant permission the first time); tapping it should bring you back
