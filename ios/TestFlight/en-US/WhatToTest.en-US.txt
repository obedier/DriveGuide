What's new in 2.16:

• Location-aware "drive-to" narration — when you start a tour far from the first stop, we now play a dynamically generated intro while you travel there, then seamlessly hand off to the pre-written tour narration the moment you arrive. A status banner at the top shows live distance + ETA.
• Follow-up narrations during long drives — adaptive cadence spaces 2-3 mini bridges across your drive so you don't sit in silence, each one with different framing (fact / sensory / pop-culture hook).
• Accurate ETAs — Google Directions API drives the "~X min" estimate instead of a straight-line approximation, so it accounts for roads and traffic.

• Featured "wAIpoint Featured" tours — new gold-standard showcase tours you can play for free. Miami's Golden Hour (2-hour driving) and Miami's Canvas (4-hour walking) are live now. Featured tours get a gold border + sparkle pill so you can tell them apart from user-shared tours at a glance.
• Instant playback for featured tours — pre-generated audio loads without the "Preparing your tour…" delay.

• "Make it your own" — every community/featured tour now has a one-tap button that clones it into your library with a "My…" title prefix, so you can edit stops or narration for your own version.
• Segment list during navigation — the in-tour player has a new ⋯ menu that opens a full list of all segments. Tap any to skip to it; checkmark, waveform, and highlight show progress. Counter ("3 of 29") always visible.
• Close button on the tour start screen — clearer way to back out without starting Simulate or Navigate.

• Fixed: community visibility toggle now sticks on the first tap instead of snapping back.
• Fixed: community tours that used to fail to load now open reliably.
• Fixed: compact Start/End toggles on Advanced search no longer truncate in portrait.

Please test:
• Start a Miami featured tour from your home. You should see a gold banner: "Heading to South Pointe Park · ~X min · tour begins when you arrive."
• Drive toward the first stop. Follow-up narrations should play roughly every 4-6 min with different openers.
