What's new in 2.10:

• Community tour library — new Top / Recent / Trending chips at the top of the Tours → Community tab. Top shows the highest-rated public tours, Trending surfaces tours with recent rating activity.
• Opt-in public visibility — a new "Visible in Community" toggle on every tour detail screen. Tours are private by default; flip the switch when you want others to see them.
• Backend: new /v1/tours/public endpoint with sort + metro filter, and a visibility endpoint for the toggle.

Please test:
• Create a tour → open its detail → flip the "Visible in Community" toggle on → go to Tours → Community → confirm it appears under "Recent"
• Flip the toggle off → confirm it disappears from Community within a few seconds
• Tap Top / Recent / Trending chips and confirm different orderings come back
• Tap any tour card in Community — should open the full tour detail
