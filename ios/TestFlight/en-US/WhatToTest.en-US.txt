What's new in 2.11:

• Route-aware narration — when you use Ferrostar turn-by-turn navigation, arriving at each stop now automatically plays the matching narration segment. No manual tap needed.
• Duplicate arrivals are ignored (re-entering the arrival radius won't re-start the segment).
• Works alongside Passenger Mode and Offline downloads — if the tour is downloaded, arrivals still trigger instantly with zero network.

Please test:
• Switch to Ferrostar navigation in Settings → Navigation engine
• Generate a short car tour, open it → Start Guided Tour → begin driving / simulating the route
• As you arrive at each stop, the at-stop narration should start automatically (within a few seconds of crossing the 80-meter arrival radius)
• Previous/Next/Pause controls still work — they just no longer require you to manually pick up after each stop
