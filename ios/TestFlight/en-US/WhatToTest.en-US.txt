What's new in 2.13:

• Fixed: the "Visible in Community" toggle on tour detail now actually stays on when you flip it. Previously the UI snapped back because the async API call completed after SwiftUI re-read the binding.
• Make it your own — featured/community tours you don't own now show a gold "Make it your own" button that clones the tour into your library with a new title prefix ("My ...") so you can edit stops and narration without affecting the original.
• Fallback for the visibility toggle: if the newer /visibility endpoint isn't deployed yet, the app transparently falls back to the existing publish/unpublish endpoints, so the toggle just works.

Please test:
• Open any tour in your library → tap "Visible in Community" → toggle should switch on, show "Visible in Community" label, and not snap back
• Toggle it off and back on a few times — no snap-backs
• Open a tour you didn't create (from Community tab) → tap "Make it your own" → confirm a new editable copy appears in your library with "My " prefix
